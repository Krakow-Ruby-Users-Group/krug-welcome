The Start KRK Up Gala party

The Start KRK Up Gala party is the celebratory conclusion of Krakow Startup Week bringing together the entire tech, startup and entrepreneurial community under a single roof for an elegant evening. The night will includes drinks, a couple short speeches (including one by the deputy mayor), a live concert and a live dj.
